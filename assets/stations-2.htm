Lovers Beach: Romantic Dining Experience At The Beach

Highlights

Description
Come dine underneath stars and see the waves crash right in front of you at Lover’s Beach—the OC's most romantic dining experience for star-crossed lovers and friends. Watch as romantic sunsets cascade around you, setting the perfect ambiance for falling (or even re-falling) in love with that special someone. Set your ears to perfectly curated live music performances featuring iconic love-inspired selections that will undoubtedly warm your soul (or your sheets). Whether you’re looking for the perfect date night or the perfect place to pick-up-a-date that night, spend your evening at SeaSalt, the home of Lover's Beach. Beyond the complimentary bottle of Alicia wine & charcuterie box included in your tickets, you will be able indulge in a hand-picked selection of curated wines & beers, incredible eats, and delectable desserts which will be available for additional purchase on-site at SeaSalt.
